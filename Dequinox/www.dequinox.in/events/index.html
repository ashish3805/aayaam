Face Off

An Alternate Reality, an epic journey of dance battles. Mind the beat, keep to the rhythm, move like magic and, most importantly, overshadow your opponent in every round and you could be the first ever winner of JUET Face-Off!

Rules & Regulations:-
1. All dance styles are allowed. (Popping Locking, Breakdance, even Classical etc.)
2. This is a classic ONE-ON-ONE dance battle event. Knockout fixtures will be created
3. There will be two rounds.
4. For the first round, participants have the option to select their own music from the bunch of tracks provided by us.
5. Solo, Duet &Group will be consider as a single category. e.g.:- a solo performer can have a battle with a group.
6. The organizing committee reserves the right to change the venue, time and rules, if required.
7. The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
8. Organizers have the right to disqualify any participant if they break any rule.

Futsal

This is a variant of association football that is played on a smaller field and mainly played indoors.

It can be considered a version of five-a-side football. Futsal is a game played between two teams of five players each, one of whom is the goalkeeper. Unlimited substitutions are permitted. Unlike some other forms of indoor football, the game is played on a hard court surface delimited by lines; walls or boards are not used. The surface, ball and rules create an emphasis on improvisation, creativity and technique as well as ball control and passing in small spaces.
