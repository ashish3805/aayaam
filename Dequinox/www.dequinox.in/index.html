About The Fest

Dequinox :The Annual Cultural Fesival of JUET acclaimed to be one the biggest festival of Central India , is a blend of competitions and frolic soirée . The 3 day festival is an eclectic collection of student performances , events , International artists , reputed bands and what not! Cherishing the legacy , this year the festival will be conducted from 3rd-5th March , 2017 .The myriad of events from Nukkad Natak to Rap Battle promises you scintillating 72 hours of unadulterated entertainment.
