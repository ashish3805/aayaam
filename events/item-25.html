PROTEAN is a multi-utility product innovation competition which encourages the students to think beyond the existing dimensional to contrive product concept.

The competition will be based on design and feasibility of the product and/or its concept. The participants can be in a team (max. 4 members) to prepare a report of their concept and/or presentation of the product to present it in the competition. The participants may also use working or static model, CAD/CAE Software, Simulation software, Animation Software to explain there concept/product.
