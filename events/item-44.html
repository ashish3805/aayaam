This is a modified version of the classic extempore where each participant will be given a topic to speak on five minutes prior to coming on stage. The twist is added where during the course of speaking, they will be hurled a bunch of impromptu prompts in the form of words, phrases or the like, which have to be included in the speaker's next few sentences, failing which will directly affect the markings. They test nothing nothing but the spontaneity of the individual and how well one can mold their thinking adaptively in creative ways.
This event will be conducted in the LT-202 on 11th of March.
