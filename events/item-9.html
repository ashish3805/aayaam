Smart vision : Making of smart Indore
The event is about spreading awareness among budding civil engineers
about infrastructure and transportation system of Indore city and making
smarter city.
It is an interactive event about taking solutions from the student
regarding problem faced by the residents in daily life, due to improper
planning, designing and inadequate functioning of citys infrastructure,
maintenance and transportation system.
