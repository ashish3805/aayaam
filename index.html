Welcome to AAYAAM

About us

Aayaam is Techno-Cultural Festival of SGSITS, Indore. The technical events provide you with an unmatched attractions ranging from hardcore technosavy events to innovative paper presentations, website designing and coding and many more. There are also some online events conducted by different branches of the college. The events are from different departments and clubs of the college that provide you with a platform to compete in the field of your choice.
The primary aim of this event is to combine information with entertainment in the form of Cultural activities

  • Concerts

    It marks a gigantic closure to this annual saga. Comprising of power-packed performances by singers, dancers and artists, setting the aura into musical vibes.

  • Competitions

    The festival witnesses number of dynamic and intriguing events, where internal as well as external participants compete for coveted titles and cash prizes.

  • Events

    Our thrilling and exciting events will leave you spellbound and insatiable. Events which will make these 2 days ecstatic and memorable. Moreover you will have chances to win goodies, gift hampers and many more exciting prizes.
